The Shadows That Linger



Worked as the Narrative Designer in a story-driven mystery game








The Shadows That Linger is a game developed by my classmates and I in University. It encapsulates a murder mystery story with the theme of miscommunication about deep-seated feelings. It possesses aspects of point-and-click adventure and resource management. The role I had on the team was mainly Lead Narrative Designer, but I had a hand in much of the ideation and implementation behind the game design.

During this project I developed the following skills:

  • How to incorporate a systematic narrative into a mystery game.
  • To have patience, possess clarity in communication, and to be stern but positive whilst giving feedback working with other writers in the team.
  • To be diligent in my communication with others on my team in order to keep the vision of the game clear and cogent.
