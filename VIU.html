VIU



Worked as the Content Manager and programmer on an augmented reality app








VIU is an augmented reality app focused in orienting parents, new students, and visitors to IU's campus. This app allows users to walk around campus in real time and tap on significant landmarks, learn more about them, and visit the corresponding Indiana University web page. In addition, users are able to walk around and examine 3D models with their camera's phone, as well as experience 360 video whilst learning about a certain part of campus.

During this project I developed the following skills:

  • How to work with and deliver clear communication to executives.
  • Developed skills importing and implementing 3D models into a Unity project.
  • To work with a full-time programmer and create an efficient pipeline for importing 3D content.
