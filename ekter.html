Ekter and The Cub



Worked as a Team Lead, Designer, and Programmer in a story-driven top-down 2D adventure game








Ekter and The Cub is a game developed by my classmates and I in my University's game development club Hoosier Games. It tells a story of redemption while delivering on top-down 2D action game elements. My teammates and I were selected to show our game at PopCon in Indianapolis, in which we set our game up at a booth and let passerbys playtest it.

During this project I developed the following skills:

  • Producer-like skills such as working in and managing a team in a project management program like HacknPlan.
  • To give clearly defined tasks with attainable deadlines to my teammates, so that no one was left with nothing to do.
  • To work with programmers, designers, and sound people and develop a sense of unity in our team.
