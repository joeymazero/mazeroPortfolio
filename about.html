About Me



I am passionate about games.




Whether it be researching new approaches to design, the state of the industry, or my favorite game, it all falls under the umbrella of my love for video games. I believe that games must be innovative while at the same time be informed of the current trends of the industry, so I go out of my way to experience all different kinds of games. Creativity can take on all forms, but it does not come from being static. I make sure as a creative I am bringing a well-rounded approach to the worlds I bring to life.
